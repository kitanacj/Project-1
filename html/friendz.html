Meet the Bratz

Sasha
Nickname: Bunny Boo

Sasha is a rockstar, not afraid to stand on business. She loves fashion and gets a lot of inspiration from music. She is always down to dance and be there for her girls when they need her.

Jade
Nickname: Kool Kat

Jade is the coolest of the cool, embracing her edgy style. She loves to explore new trends and make new outfits everyday. She can't wait to go on her next adventure and come up with some kool designs.

Cloe
Nickname: Angel

Cloe is the one full of energy, she finds her fun in doing sports and staying active. She enjoys being with her friends and always taking pictures to keep memories alive.

Yasmin
Nickname: Pretty Princess

Yasmin is very shy and quiet. She has many hobbies that involve writing, fashion, and is an animal lover. She is always down for a great time with her best friends.
